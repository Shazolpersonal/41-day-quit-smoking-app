## Quick Reference

### Google Play Store Requirements

#### App Descriptions
- **Short description**: 80 characters max
- **Full description**: 4000 characters max
- **Languages**: Bengali (primary), English (secondary)

#### Screenshots
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Size**: 1080 x 1920 px (portrait recommended)
- **Count**: 2-8 screenshots
- **File size**: 8 MB max per screenshot

#### Feature Graphic
- **Size**: 1024 x 500 px
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **File size**: 1 MB max
- **Required**: Yes (for featured placement)

#### App Icon
- **Size**: 512 x 512 px
- **Format**: PNG (32-bit)
- **File size**: 1 MB max
- **Required**: Yes

#### Privacy Policy
- **Required**: Yes
- **Must be hosted**: On accessible URL
- **Languages**: Match app languages

### Apple App Store Requirements

#### App Descriptions
- **Subtitle**: 30 characters max
- **Description**: 4000 characters max
- **Promotional text**: 170 characters max

#### Screenshots
- **iPhone**: 1242 x 2688 px, 1284 x 2778 px
- **iPad**: 2048 x 2732 px
- **Count**: 1-10 screenshots per device type

#### App Icon
- **Size**: 1024 x 1024 px
- **Format**: PNG (no transparency)
- **Required**: Yes
