## Introduction

Welcome to ৪১ দিনে ধূমপান ত্যাগ (41 Day Quit Smoking). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Our app stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

#### Progress Data
- Smoke-free days count
- Money saved calculations
- Health improvement milestones
- Achievement badges earned

#### Journal Entries
- Daily journal entries
- Mood tracking data
- Craving logs
- Trigger analysis notes

#### Settings & Preferences
- Notification preferences
- Prayer time settings
- Location for prayer times (if permission granted)
- App theme preferences
- Language preferences

#### Security Data
- PIN code (encrypted)
- Biometric authentication settings
- Security preferences

### Data We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we **do not** collect:

❌ We do NOT collect any personal information on our servers
❌ We do NOT track your location beyond prayer time calculations
❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties
❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
❌ We do NOT display advertisements
❌ We do NOT require account creation or login
❌ We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other apps

## Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

### Required Permissions

#### Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: Save your progress, journal entries, and settings
- **Usage**: Store data locally on your device
- **Can be denied**: No, required for app functionality

#### Notifications Permission
- **Purpose**: Send reminders and motivational messages
- **Usage**: Daily task reminders, prayer time alerts, milestone celebrations
- **Can be denied**: Yes, but you'll miss important reminders

### Optional Permissions

#### Location Permission
- **Purpose**: Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
- **Usage**: One-time location fetch for prayer time calculations
- **Can be denied**: Yes, app will use default location (Dhaka, Bangladesh)
- **Note**: We do NOT track or store your location continuously

#### Biometric Permission
- **Purpose**: Unlock app with fingerprint or face recognition
- **Usage**: Alternative to PIN lock
- **Can be denied**: Yes, you can use PIN instead
